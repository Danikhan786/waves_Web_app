Privacy Policy

Introduction

We at Waves are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of personal information we collect, how we use it, and the measures we take to ensure your information is protected.

Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: When you create an account, we may collect personal details such as your name, email address, phone number, and payment information.
  • Usage Data: We collect data on how you use the app, including ride bookings, preferences, and interactions within the app.
  • Location Data:To provide location-based services, we may collect and process information about your actual location.

How We Use Your Information

  • To Provide Services:Your information helps us deliver the water travel services you request, such as booking a ride or engaging in water activities.
  • For Improvement:We analyze usage data to improve the app’s functionality, user experience, and security.
  • Communication:We may use your contact information to send you updates, promotional offers, or important notices about the app.

Data Sharing and Disclosure

  • Third-Party Service Providers:We may share your information with trusted third parties who help us operate the app and provide our services, such as payment processors and customer support.

Data Security

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information. You can manage your privacy settings within the app or contact us directly for assistance.

Changes to This Policy
